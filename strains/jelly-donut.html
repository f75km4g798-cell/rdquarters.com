Featured Profile

Jelly Donut

Indica-Dominant Hybrid

Jelly Donut is commonly described as a dessert-style hybrid combining fruity sweetness, dough-like flavor and a relaxing character that may become heavier as the experience develops.

Commonly Reported Lineage

Purple Punch × French Toast.

General Classification

Commonly categorized as an indica-dominant hybrid.

Aroma

Sour berry, grape, sweet dough, sage and earthy or herbal notes.

Flavor

Sweet and doughy with berry, grape, citrus and lightly herbal qualities.

Commonly Reported Effects

Relaxing Euphoric Giggly Hungry Potentially Sleepy

Terpene Tendencies

Without laboratory testing, an exact terpene profile cannot be confirmed. Published descriptions commonly associate Jelly Donut with combinations of pinene, caryophyllene and myrcene. The actual balance may vary by grower, phenotype and harvest.

Pinene Caryophyllene Myrcene

Experience Notes

Jelly Donut is commonly associated with a cheerful or euphoric beginning followed by increasing physical relaxation.

Some users describe appetite stimulation, laughter and sleepiness, making it commonly associated with later-day or evening settings.

Individual experiences may differ based on tolerance, amount used, product form, cultivation method and personal response.
