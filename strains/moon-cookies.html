Featured Profile

Moon Cookies

Indica-Dominant Hybrid

Moon Cookies is commonly described as a Cookies-family cultivar known for a rich dessert-like character and an experience that may begin mentally uplifting before gradually becoming more physically relaxing.

Commonly Reported Lineage

Often described as a Girl Scout Cookies backcross. Genetics may vary between breeders and cultivators.

General Classification

Commonly categorized as an indica-dominant hybrid.

Aroma

Sweet dough, earth, citrus, berry and subtle skunky or forest-like notes.

Flavor

Sweet and earthy with cookie-like, citrus, berry and lightly sour notes.

Commonly Reported Effects

Uplifting Euphoric Mentally Engaging Physically Relaxing Potentially Sedating

Terpene Tendencies

Without a laboratory report, no exact terpene profile can be confirmed. Cookies-family cultivars are often associated with combinations of caryophyllene, limonene and myrcene, but the actual balance may vary considerably between growers, phenotypes and harvests.

Caryophyllene Limonene Myrcene

Experience Notes

Moon Cookies is commonly described as beginning with an uplifting or euphoric mental character before its heavier physical qualities become more noticeable.

Because the relaxing qualities may increase over time, many descriptions associate it with later-day or evening settings.

Individual experience may differ based on personal tolerance, amount used, cultivation method, phenotype and product form.
